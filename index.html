About Despinas Mare Hotel

Despina’s Mare is situated just 3-4 minutes walk from the cosmopolitan center of Naoussa.

We are a family business with years of experience and we offer high quality services in a a quiet, friendly and welcoming environment. Despina's Mare, built according to the cycladic architecture, is located at the most beautiful location of Naoussa, just 300m from the center and 150m from the wonderful beach of Agioi Anargyri.

In the interior yard - patio, you can enjoy and live moments of tranquility and relaxation. From your balcony, you can enjoy the unique sea view, the sunset and the picturesque port with the Venetian castle. Our staff is always at your disposal to provide you with all the necessary information for discovering Paros and enjoying your holidays..

The nice and fully renovated rooms, the unique view to the traditional port of Naoussa and the warm hospitality, will make your stay memorable!

    Discover Paros island

    Paros is a captivating Greek island in the heart of the Cyclades, offering a wonderful blend of natural beauty, historical charm, and vibrant culture.

    Paros boasts some of the most stunning beaches in the Aegean. From the popular Golden Beach with its golden sands and water sports to the more secluded and picturesque Kolymbithres, there's a beach for every preference. The island is dotted with traditional Cycladic villages characterized by narrow alleys, white-washed houses adorned with bougainvillea, and vibrant blue-domed churches. Naoussa and Parikia are two particularly charming towns to explore. Naoussa, a picturesque fishing village in the northern part of the island, is known for its charming harbor, traditional Greek architecture, and vibrant nightlife. The island is renowned for its excellent wind conditions, making it a popular destination for windsurfers and kitesurfers. Pounda Beach, in particular, is famous for its water sports facilities.
